No more wandering around! 
Imagine walking into a big room with many seats. And having to keep looking around to find 
available seats. Instead, with spotter, by just a quick glance at a screen, you can see 
green squares for available seats and red squares for taken ones. It's the modern solution 
for simple and hassle free seating.In addition, it also features camera recordings and 
anti-cheat measures that track hand and head movement. To make your experience even more
better there's an analytics option that let's you know peak times and popular spots. 
It's not just about finding seats, it's about enhancing the entire experience!
