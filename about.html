No more wandering around!
Imagine walking into a big room with many seats. And having to keep looking around to find available seats. Instead, with Spotter, by just a quick glance at a screen, you can see green squares for available seats and red squares for taken ones. It's the modern solution for simple and hassle-free seating. In addition, it also features camera recordings and anti-cheat measures that track hand and head movement. To make your experience even better, there's an analytics option that lets you know peak times and popular spots. It's not just about finding seats; it's about enhancing the entire experience!
